Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

  • Why didn't my purchase show up in the app? If your premium features or points do not refresh after purchasing, please try logging out and logging back in. Alternatively, contact support with a screenshot of your purchase receipt, and we will manually apply it for you immediately.
  • How do I scan a receipt? Tap the camera icon at the bottom of the main screen to start scanning. Simply point your camera at any physical Japanese receipt ("領収書"), and our AI assistant will automatically recognize, categorize, and log the expense for you.
